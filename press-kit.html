Professional bio

Built in the culture. Trained for the business.

Shelbi Johnson-Wakefield, professionally known as Shelbi Massaratti and “The Real Miss Texas,” is the founder and manager behind Plattinum Management. Her entertainment work dates to 2001 and spans artist development, booking, promotion, live events, independent music, broadcasting, brand development and practical artist advocacy.

Her A.A.S. in Paralegal Studies from San Antonio College adds a contract-aware business perspective to hands-on experience in artist strategy, communications, promotion and event logistics. That perspective supports clearer organization and expectations; it is not legal representation.

As a recording artist, Shelbi released the 2021 single “Lolli Pop,” featuring Hakeem Kydavii. Her media work includes Pink TalkO Radio, launched in 2020.

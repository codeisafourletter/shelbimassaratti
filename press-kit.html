Professional biography

A career built across entertainment, business, music and media.

Shelbi Johnson-Wakefield, professionally known as Shelbi Massaratti and “The Real Miss Texas,” is a San Antonio-based entertainment professional and the founder and manager behind Plattinum Management. Her work spans artist development, booking, promotion, live events, independent music, broadcasting, branding and practical artist advocacy.

Her entertainment work dates to 2001. Across more than two decades, she has worked with emerging talent, built a Texas-based management and promotional identity, launched Pink TalkO Radio, released independent music under her own name and promoted live entertainment.

Shelbi earned an A.A.S. in Paralegal Studies from San Antonio College. Current research materials also reflect honors recognition and Phi Theta Kappa membership. Her studies add research, writing, documentation and contract-aware organization to hands-on entertainment experience. They do not make Shelbi or Plattinum Management a legal-services provider.

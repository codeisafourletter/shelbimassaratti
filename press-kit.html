Biography

Management, music and media from an independent point of view.

Shelbi Johnson-Wakefield, professionally known as Shelbi Massaratti and The Real Miss Texas, is a San Antonio-based artist manager, promoter and founder of Plattinum Management. Her work supports independent rap and hip-hop artists through artist direction, release planning, booking, promotion, events, branding, media and practical follow-through. Shelbi’s perspective is shaped by hands-on work across management, live entertainment, independent music and public-facing media. She launched Pink TalkO as a media project in 2020 and released “Lolli Pop” featuring Hakeem Kydavii in 2021, giving her firsthand experience with the demands of presenting and promoting work under her own name. She also earned an A.A.S. in Paralegal Studies from San Antonio College, strengthening the research, writing, documentation and organization she brings to her business work.

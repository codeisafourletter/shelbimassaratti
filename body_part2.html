03 / About Shelbi

I know both sides of the work.

I’m Shelbi Johnson-Wakefield, professionally known as Shelbi Massaratti and The Real Miss Texas. I’m based in San Antonio and built Plattinum Management to help independent artists move with more direction.

I’ve worked across management, promotion, events, media and independent music. I know what gets missed when nobody is handling the details.

That range helps me see the full picture: how the artist is presented, how the release is supported, what the show needs and whether the team is actually following through.

I also earned an A.A.S. in Paralegal Studies from San Antonio College. The research, writing and organization from that education sharpen how I document details and communicate around the business.

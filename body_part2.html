03 / About

Built in the culture. Trained for the business.

Shelbi Johnson-Wakefield, professionally known as Shelbi Massaratti and “The Real Miss Texas,” is a San Antonio-based artist manager, promoter and the force behind Plattinum Management.

She knows the gap between making a record and building a career—and works in the space between the two.

Her entertainment work dates to 2001 and spans artist management, booking, promotion, live events, independent music, broadcasting, brand development and practical artist advocacy. Across every lane, her work has stayed grounded in relationships, execution and momentum.

Her A.A.S. in Paralegal Studies from San Antonio College adds a contract-aware business perspective to years of hands-on work in promotion, communications, event logistics and independent creative work.

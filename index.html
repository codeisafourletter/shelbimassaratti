03 / About

Built in the culture. Trained for the business.

Shelbi Johnson-Wakefield, professionally known as Shelbi Massaratti and “The Real Miss Texas,” is a San Antonio-based artist manager, promoter and the force behind Plattinum Management.

She knows the gap between making a record and building a career—and works in the space between the two.

Her entertainment work dates to 2001 and spans artist management, booking, promotion, live events, independent music, broadcasting, brand development and practical artist advocacy. The through-line is not celebrity proximity; it is an ability to build relationships, create momentum and keep the work moving.

Her A.A.S. in Paralegal Studies from San Antonio College adds a contract-aware business perspective uncommon in grassroots entertainment management. That training sits beside years of hands-on experience in promotion, communications, event logistics and independent creative work.

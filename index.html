01 / The Bio

Business instinct. Creative nerve.

Shelbi Johnson-Wakefield, professionally known as Shelbi Massaratti, is a Texas-based entertainment professional and the force behind Plattinum Management. Her work spans artist development, booking, promotion, live media and independent music.

She connects the stage, the strategy and the story—helping emerging artists move with more direction, visibility and confidence.

With formal paralegal training from San Antonio College, Shelbi brings a contract-aware perspective to entertainment work. Her approach combines relationship-building, grassroots promotion, event coordination and practical artist advocacy.

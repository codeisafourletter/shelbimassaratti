01 / What I Do

Music needs movement.

I work across the parts of an independent career that have to connect: direction, presentation, relationships, bookings, promotion and follow-through.

01

Artist Direction

We get honest about the records, the priorities and the next move. I bring structure and direct feedback without sanding down who you are.

02

Release Planning

I help map the single, timing, content and promotion so release day is part of a campaign—not the end of one.

03

Shows & Booking

Venue outreach, appearances, event coordination and preparation for the rooms that make sense for the artist.

04

Brand & Content

Clear positioning, a recognizable look, sharper messaging and promotional pieces that match the music.

05

Promotion & Media

Relationship-based opportunities, media angles, event support and collaborations when the timing and fit are real.

06

Follow-Through

Calendars, communication, details and accountability. Good opportunities fall apart when nobody owns the next step.

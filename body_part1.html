01 / Her Story

A career bigger than one title.

Shelbi’s history crosses management, booking, promotion, events, music, broadcasting, branding and artist advocacy. Plattinum Management is one result of that career—not the whole story.

Shelbi built her name by moving between the stage, the strategy, the audience and the business behind the work.

Her entertainment work dates to 2001. Over the years, she has developed artists, promoted shows, coordinated opportunities, built media platforms, released music under her own name and created a recognizable Texas identity as “The Real Miss Texas.”

Her career combines cultural instinct with formal business training. That combination is what makes her profile unusual: she understands the creative pressure because she has carried it, and she understands the organizational side because she has studied and practiced it.
